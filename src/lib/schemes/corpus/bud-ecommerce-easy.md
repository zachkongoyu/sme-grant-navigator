# BUD Fund (E-commerce Easy) — Overview

BUD E-commerce Easy is a streamlined BUD track for SMEs building or enhancing online sales channels for approved export markets (Mainland China and FTA/IPPA economies).

**Key facts:**

- Project funding ceiling: **HK$800,000**
- Duration: up to **24 months**
- Focus: e-commerce platform buildout, digital marketing for online sales, and related online sales enablement activities
- Follows the same reimbursement mechanics and concurrent/cumulative caps as other BUD tracks
- Self-implementation and HK-entity-execution requirements apply

E-commerce Easy is best suited for enterprises whose primary market-entry vehicle is an online channel — e.g. establishing a flagship store on Tmall or Lazada, or building a Mainland-compatible e-commerce site.
