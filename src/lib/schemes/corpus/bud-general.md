# BUD Fund (General Application) — Overview

BUD Fund General Application supports larger-scale market development and branding projects for Hong Kong enterprises entering Mainland China or approved FTA/IPPA economies.

**Key facts:**

- Project funding ceiling: **HK$800,000**
- Matching ratio: 1 government : 1 enterprise (50/50), or 1:2 for some activity types — verify current ratio at www.bud.hkpc.org
- Duration: up to **24 months**
- Unlike Easy BUD, you can engage **external service providers** in the target market (Type i activities)
- A **20% upfront payment** option is available after approval
- Processing time: approximately 60+ working days
- Same concurrent cap (HK$800,000) and cumulative cap (HK$7,000,000) as Easy BUD apply across all BUD tracks

General Application suits enterprises running larger, more complex projects that need external partners in the target market, or projects requiring more than 12 months to execute.
