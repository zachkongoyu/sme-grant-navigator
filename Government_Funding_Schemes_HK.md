## Carbon Reduction

### 1. Cleaner Production Partnership Programme

**Objective:** Encourage and facilitate Hong Kong-owned factories in Hong Kong/Guangdong Province to adopt cleaner production technologies and practices.

**Target Recipients:** Enterprises registered in Hong Kong and owning and operating a factory in Hong Kong/Guangdong.

**Administering Body:** Hong Kong Productivity Council  
**Tel:** 2788 5588  
**Email:** enquiry@cleanerproduction.hk  
**Website:** https://www.cleanerproduction.hk/en/

---

### 2. Dedicated Fund on Branding, Upgrading and Domestic Sales (BUD Fund)

**Objective:** Provide funding support to non-listed Hong Kong enterprises to enhance their competitiveness and facilitate business development in the Chinese Mainland and all other economies with which Hong Kong has signed Free Trade Agreements (FTAs) and/or Investment Promotion and Protection Agreements (IPPAs) through developing brands, upgrading and restructuring operations and promoting sales.

**Target Recipients:** Non-listed Hong Kong enterprises with substantive business operations in Hong Kong.

> **Note:** The 40 economies currently covered under the BUD Fund include the Chinese Mainland, New Zealand, the four Member States of the European Free Trade Association, Chile, Macao, the ten Member States of ASEAN, Georgia, Australia, Austria, Belgo-Luxembourg Economic Union, Canada, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Korea, Kuwait, Mexico, the Netherlands, Sweden, the United Arab Emirates, the United Kingdom, Türkiye, Bahrain and Peru.

**Administering Body:** Hong Kong Productivity Council  
**Tel:** 2788 6088  
**Email:** bud_sec@hkpc.org  
**Website:** https://www.bud.hkpc.org/en

### 5. Fisheries Development Loan Fund

**Objective:** Provide loans to capture fishermen and owners of fish collectors for switching to more sustainable fisheries operations and other fisheries-related operations, and for carrying out projects that will reduce fuel consumption or carbon footprint of their operations (without increasing fishing effort); and provide loans to fish farmers to help them develop aquaculture or improve their aquaculture businesses leading to more environmentally friendly operations or an improvement of the quality and food safety standards of the aquaculture products.

**Target Recipients:** Hong Kong residents and local fishing vessel or fish collector owners.

**Administering Body:** Agriculture, Fisheries and Conservation Department

**Objective:** Help SMEs and non-listed enterprises obtain financing from participating lenders for meeting business needs.

### 24. Innovation and Technology Support Programme (ITSP) under Innovation and Technology Fund

**Objective:**

- **ITSP (Platform & Seed):** Support applied R&D projects undertaken by R&D Centres or designated local public research institutes with a view to transferring the R&D results to local industries.
- **ITSP (Collaborative) (formerly the Partnership Research Programme (PRP)):** Support applied R&D projects undertaken by R&D Centres or designated local public research institutes in collaboration with companies.

- **ITSP (Platform & Seed):** R&D Centres or designated local public research institutes.

## Facilitating Technology Adoption

### 28. Public Sector Trial Scheme (PSTS) under Innovation and Technology Fund

## Traffic-related

### 38. Smart Traffic Fund

**Objective:** Provide funding support to local organisations or enterprises for conducting research and application of innovation and technology with the objectives of enhancing commuting convenience, enhancing efficiency of the road network or road space, and improving driving safety.

**Target Recipients:**
